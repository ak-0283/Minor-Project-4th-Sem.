How It Works

Our advanced AI emotion detection system uses cutting-edge neural networks to analyze facial expressions with exceptional accuracy.

AI Processing Pipeline

Face Detection

Locates all faces in the image using a cascading convolutional network with 98.7% accuracy.

Landmark Detection

Maps 43 facial landmarks to identify key features including eyes, eyebrows, mouth, and jawline.

Feature Extraction

Calculates distances and relationships between landmarks to create a feature vector for analysis.

Neural Classification

Deep learning model analyzes feature vectors and classifies emotions based on training data of 500,000+ facial expressions.

Result Delivery

Summarizes findings with confidence scores, creates visualizations, and delivers real-time analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our emotion detection technology.

Our emotion detection system achieves 98.3% accuracy on standardized datasets. The technology uses a deep convolutional neural network trained on over 500,000 diverse facial images to ensure high reliability across different ethnicities, ages, and lighting conditions. The system detects 7 primary emotions (happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, fear, disgust, and neutral) and can identify complex emotional states by analyzing micro-expressions.

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All processing happens locally in your browser using client-side TensorFlow.js, meaning your images and video data never leave your device or get uploaded to our servers. We don't store any facial data, analysis results, or personally identifiable information. Our technology is fully GDPR and CCPA compliant, giving you complete control and privacy while using our services.

Our emotion detection technology works on most modern devices and browsers. For optimal performance, we recommend using Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari (latest versions). Mobile support includes iOS 13+ and Android 8+. The system is responsive and adapts to different screen sizes, from desktops to smartphones. For webcam functionality, you'll need a device with a camera and permission to access it. Performance may vary based on your device's processing power, with faster results on newer devices.

Yes, our technology can detect and analyze emotions in multiple faces simultaneously. The system can track up to 20 faces in a single image or video frame, depending on your device's processing capability. Each detected face receives its own individual emotion analysis with separate confidence scores. This makes our technology ideal for analyzing group dynamics, audience reactions, or multiple participants in various settings.

Our emotion detection typically processes results in 150-300 milliseconds per frame, depending on your device's capabilities. This enables real-time analysis at 30+ frames per second on modern devices. The optimized TensorFlow.js model uses WebGL acceleration when available to maximize performance. For static images, results appear almost instantly, while webcam streams provide continuous real-time feedback, allowing you to see emotion changes as they happen.
